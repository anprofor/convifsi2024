Pablo Barrera


Pablo Barrera has over 20 years of experience in cybersecurity and technology delivering projects all over the world. He is the founder of ES Consulting, a cybersecurity consulting firm that helps organizations become safer, more agile, and resilient. Currently he oversees the Cybersecurity Services area in the company and manages the team that delivers over a 100 cybersecurity related projects per year around the world. A university professor for two well-known universities in his country.

Pablo is a member of the ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC 27 Information security, cybersecurity and privacy protection technical committee, board member of the ISC2 Local Chapter and president of the local OWASP Chapter. He holds certifications like CISSP, CEH, ISO 27001 Lead Auditor, ISO 27035 Lead Incident Manager, ISO 27032 Cybersecurity Manager, and many other, authorized trainer for PECB and Mile2 certifications.

He was selected as Professional Fellow by the U.S. Department of State in 2015, in 2014 he was selected as a TEDxChange Fellow by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. He has published a CVE and collaborated in several cybersecurity research projects.

Pablo Barrera tiene más de 20 años de experiencia en ciberseguridad y tecnología, llevando a cabo proyectos alrededor del mundo. Es el fundador de ES Consulting, estudio ingeniería en sistemas en Universidad Galileo y actualmente es catedrático de la maestría en Ciberseguridad, la maestría de Auditoría de Sistemas, y director del área de servicios de ciberseguridad de ES Consulting.

Pablo es miembro del comité técnico ISO/IEC JTC 1/SC27 de Seguridad de Información, Ciberseguridad y protección de la privacidad, miembro de la junta directiva local de ISC2, presidente de OWASP Guatemala y posee certificaciones en ciberseguridad de renombre mundial. Fue seleccionado como Fellow en el 2014 por la fundación Bill y Melinda Gates y en 2015 como Professional Fellow por el Departamento de Estado de Estados Unidos. Ha realizado investigaciones sobre vulnerabilidades de infraestructura y ciberseguridad.
